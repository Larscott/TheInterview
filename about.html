About the Movie

The Interview is a controversial 2014 American comedy film about a celebrity jounalist and his producer, Dave Skylark (James Franco) and Aaron Rapoport (Seth Rogan), who land an interview with North Korean dictator Kim Jung-Un (Randall Park) and then go on a mission to assassinate him. They are perhaps the two least-qualified men imaginable to assassinate Kim Jong-Un.
